How to make Fried Rice

This is how i make my fried rice

Ingredients

  • Rice (cooked a day before)
  • Chives
  • Chile pepper
  • onion
  • garlic
  • eggs
  • beef
  • oil vegetables
  • soy sauce
  • oyster sauce
  • salt

Steps

  1. Step 1:Chop and Prepare the Ingredients
  2. Step 2: Season the Meat and prepare the egg and rice
  3. Step 3: Mix the Ingredients
  4. Step 4: Add Soy sauce, Oyster sauce and salt to taste
  5. Step 5: Serve
